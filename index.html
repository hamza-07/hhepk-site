Our Services

  • Corrugated carton

    Corrugated cartons are constructed from multiple layers, unlike single-sheet cardboard. They consist of three layers: an inner liner, an outer liner, and a middle layer with a fluted structure in between, which provides added strength and durability.

  • Packaging

    Packaging protects products from damage, preserves freshness, and enhances branding. It offers convenience, provides essential information, and promotes sustainability with eco-friendly materials.

  • Textile Paper Cone

    Textile paper cones provide strong, eco-friendly support for winding yarn and thread. They ensure smooth handling during production, protect the yarn from damage, and are fully recyclable.

  • Paper tube

    Paper tubes offer durable, eco-friendly support for storing and transporting products like fabrics, posters, and industrial materials. They provide protection, structure, and are fully recyclable.

  • Printed core

    Printed cores provide strong support for winding materials like paper or textiles, while offering branding opportunities with custom logos or designs.

  • Unit Boxes

    Unit boxes are compact packaging solutions designed to protect individual products, enhance branding, and ensure easy handling during storage and transport.
